Mô tả Công việc


To maximize returns on HVB's Brand investments by developing clear channel & customer strategies and execution plans that (i) help Sales teams to profitably execute the brand plans and (ii) maximize brand availability & visibility at point-of-purchase.


Also the Trade Marketing Manager are expected to

a. Agree and align on key brand objectives with Sales and ensure these are delivered in line with plan.

b. Support Sales with tools to optimize in-trade execution of brand strategies.

c. Prioritize which channels & partners will deliver best long-term returns.

d. Make HVB the preferred drinks supplier for our retail & wholesale partners


* All the other detail will be disscussed in interview


Yêu Cầu Công Việc


Minimum of 5 years experience.

Bachelor degree in business.

Passionate and committed to achieving results

Well versed in working with both sales and marketing areas

Team working

Strong cross cultural understanding and awareness

Good networking and negotiating skills

Intimate knowledge of the region, market, trade associations, business associates.

Understanding of the valued chain of the company

Open minded
